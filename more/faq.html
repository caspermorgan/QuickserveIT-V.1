❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services, pricing, and process.

Common Questions

What is your turnaround time?

Our average turnaround time is 24 hours. For urgent projects, we offer priority service with 12-hour or even 6-hour turnaround times at slightly higher rates. Contact us for specific deadlines.

What is your accuracy guarantee?

We guarantee 99% accuracy on all projects. Every document is double-checked before delivery. If errors are found, we provide free corrections until you're satisfied.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes! We offer discounts based on project size: 2000-5000 words (5% OFF), 5000-10,000 words (10% OFF), 10,000+ words (20% OFF). Contact us for custom bulk pricing.

How do I pay?

We accept multiple payment methods: WhatsApp Pay, UPI, Bank Transfer, Google Pay, and Paytm. Payment is typically 50% advance, 50% on delivery. Monthly packages can be paid after project completion.

Do you handle confidential work?

Yes, absolutely. All client work is kept strictly confidential. We sign NDAs when required. Your privacy and data security are our top priorities.

Can I request revisions?

Yes! We provide unlimited revisions until you're 100% satisfied. If changes are requested, we update the project and resubmit within 24 hours.

Do you offer monthly packages?

Yes! We offer customized monthly packages for schools and content creators. Schools: ₹5,000-15,000/month. Creators: ₹15,000-40,000/month. Contact us for custom quotes.

What file formats do you work with?

We work with all common formats: Word (.docx), PDF, Excel (.xlsx), Google Docs, [translate:हिंदी] Hindi documents, and more. Just let us know your preferred format.
